FAQ

What is a TimeBuyer?
He/She is a person who need another person made something and he/she want to pay for this.
What is a TimeSeller?
He/She is a person who have free time and want to ear some money doing something for others.
Can I be a Timebuyer and a TimeSeller at the same time?
Sure. Sometimes you need time from others and other times you can sell your time to others.
How can I be a TimeBuyer?
